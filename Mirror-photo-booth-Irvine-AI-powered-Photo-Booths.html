The Magic of AI: Ditch the Green Screen

Traditional photo booths often rely on physical green screens to remove and replace backgrounds. This can be cumbersome and sometimes leads to inconsistent results. Our AI Background Changer, however, uses advanced AI technology to seamlessly separate subjects from their backgrounds in real-time, all without the need for a physical green screen. This means a faster, more reliable, and more professional experience for everyone.


Key Features of the AI Background Changer

  • Offline Functionality: Our custom-trained AI model performs background removal directly on the photo booth's computer. This means you don't have to worry about a spotty internet connection affecting the experience. The process is quick, reliable, and provides instant results.
  • Professional-Quality Backdrops: Guests can choose from a wide variety of high-quality virtual backdrops that can be customized to fit your event's theme. You can even set the backgrounds to change automatically, adding an extra layer of fun and surprise.
  • Custom Branding: Make the experience your own with custom-branded overlays, templates, and designs. This is a fantastic way to promote your brand at corporate events or personalize a wedding or birthday party.
  • Expert Assistance: Every AI Background Changer rental includes a Creative Director and Attendant to assist guests, ensuring everything runs smoothly. We also handle the free on-site setup and breakdown in Los Angeles and Orange County.
  • Instant Social Sharing: Our enhanced social sharing technology makes it easy for guests to instantly share their photos to social media, giving your event a wider reach and creating a viral buzz.
